About Me

Hi there! I'm Monirul Islam, a tech enthusiastic guy who is ready to take challenges. I have completed my MSc in Department of Computer Science at Shizuoka University.

I have been working with Unity3D game engine for almost 5 years, developing prototypes for Games, Augmented Reality, and Virtual Reality applications. My research focuses on "Human Agent Interaction".
